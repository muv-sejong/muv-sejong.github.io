Student Research Tracks

Students can join theory-driven, software-driven, or mission-driven research tracks.

01

Control Theory

Lyapunov theory, nonlinear control, robust control, sliding-mode control, sampled-data systems, and stability analysis.

02

Marine Autonomy

UUV and USV guidance, path following, formation control, cooperative mission operation, and marine vehicle dynamics.

03

Simulation and Software

MATLAB, Simulink, Python, C/C++, ROS2, digital twin simulation, and autonomy verification.

04

Defense AI

Mission planning, world models, decision support, distributed autonomy, and AI-assisted autonomous operation.
