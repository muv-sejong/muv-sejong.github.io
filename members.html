Principal Investigator

Dowan Kim

Professor, Department of Defense Systems Engineering
Sejong University, Seoul, Republic of Korea

Professor Dowan Kim received the B.S., M.S., and Ph.D. degrees in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Yonsei University, Korea, in 2002, 2004, and 2007, respectively. He was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of California, Berkeley in 2008 and a research professor at Yonsei University in 2009. Before joining Sejong University in 2025, he served as a professor at Hanbat National University.
