Ongoing Projects

Ongoing · LIG Nex1 · 2025–2027

Cooperative Operation Technology for Mine-Detection UUVs

Development of guidance, control, cooperative operation, and autonomous mission technologies for multiple unmanned underwater vehicles under underwater communication constraints.

Ongoing · NRF · 2025–2028

Hybrid Path-Following Approach for Unmanned Underwater Vehicles Using Nonlinear Model-Based and Data-Driven Control

Theoretical and experimental validation of a hybrid path-following approach for unmanned underwater vehicles combining nonlinear model-based control and data-driven control.

Ongoing · Lab Platform

Marine Unmanned Vehicle Digital Twin Simulation Platform

Development of a mission-level simulation platform for marine unmanned systems considering path following, cooperative operation, communication delay, packet loss, and actuator constraints.

Ongoing · Lab Platform

Guidance-and-Control-Centered Autonomy Testbed

Operation of a common laboratory testbed for verifying guidance-and-control-centered autonomy algorithms using MATLAB, Python, and simulation-based validation.
