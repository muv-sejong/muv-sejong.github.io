Research

Guidance-and-control-centered marine autonomy.

Guidance, control, simulation, and autonomy for unmanned underwater vehicles, unmanned surface vehicles, and defense-oriented marine systems.

01

Nonlinear Control for Autonomy

Lyapunov-based control, sliding-mode control, sampled-data control, input saturation, robustness, and stability analysis for autonomous unmanned systems.

02

Guidance and Path Following

Guidance laws, path-following control, trajectory tracking, waypoint missions, and 3D maneuvering for unmanned underwater and surface vehicles.

03

Cooperative Marine Autonomy

Formation control, cooperative path following, distributed coordination, and autonomous operation under delayed, intermittent, and low-rate underwater communication.

04

Mission-Level Simulation

6-DOF vehicle dynamics, digital twin simulation, mission scenario generation, performance evaluation, and verification of autonomy algorithms.
